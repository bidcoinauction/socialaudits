HOW WE DO IT

Baseline → anomaly → fingerprint

We continuously monitor topics across major social platforms, alongside news and market data. For each topic, we learn what “normal” looks like, then flag the moment attention breaks pattern. From there, we fingerprint the spread—showing who amplified it, how it moved across communities and regions, and whether it appears organic or coordinated.

1 Monitor

Ongoing topic monitoring across social, news, and market data.

2 Learn

Adaptive baselines that reflect normal behavior for each topic.

3 Detect

Early alerts when attention meaningfully deviates from baseline.

4 Explain

Diffusion and coordination fingerprints, linked to news and price/volume.

Key point: early isn’t enough. The signal must be explainable and verifiable.
